# Polito - PM Lab
Project Management Lab @ Polytechnic University of Turin.

The PM Lab promotes the creation and dissemination of practical knowledge in **Project Management (PM)** and **Project Portfolio Management (PPM)**.

The Lab is actively engaged in **action research on the application of Artificial Intelligence to Project Management**, including **forecasting and estimate-at-completion**, decision support, and performance analytics. Key research areas also include **Project Management Information Systems (PMIS)**, data architectures, and **system integrations** supporting end-to-end project governance.

This repository collects research artifacts, prototypes, and experiments related to traditional, Agile, and Hybrid PM approaches, with a strong focus on data-driven and AI-enabled project management practices.
